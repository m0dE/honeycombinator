What is this?

    Honey Combinator is a proof of concept where you can browse Y Combinator website in a Massively Multiplayer Online (MMO) game world.
    I thought it'd be interesting to see website visitors interact with each other.
    This project is not affiliated with Y Combinator, and the content & characters depicted are based on the author's imagination.

Can you make me a custom MMO game for my website?

    Yes! tell me about your website. I'm willing to make a game for free if I can get exposure through your website.

How do I play?
  • Move - Right Click
  • Talk to NPC's - Left Click
  • Chat - Enter
