What is this?

    This webpage exists to demonstrate how a HTML5 canvas MMO game can be embedded into a website using modd.io
    Honey Combinator is an unofficial game to explore Y Combinator's website. The characters depicted in this game are based off of the author's limited knowledge

How do I play?
    Goal

      Collect as much honey as possible and build a successful hive

    Commands
    • Move to position - Mouse left click or W A S D
    • Interact with NPC's - Mouse right click or Space
    • Chat with other players - Enter
    • Open Hacker News - N
