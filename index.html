What is this?

    This is a demonstration of using a MMO game to interact with web content.
    Honey Combinator is an unofficial game made for exploring Y Combinator. The characters depicted in this game are completely based off of the author's imagination and nothing more.

How do I play?
    Commands
    • Move to position - Mouse right click or W A S D
    • Interact with NPC's - Mouse left click or Space
    • Chat with other players - Enter
    Goal

      Go to forest and collect honey
