What is this?

    Honey Combinator is an unofficial client for browsing Hacker News + Y combinator using a MMO game engine (modd.io).

Why are you doing this?

    In here, visitors can see and interact with each other which was not possible in the conventional websites.

    This is an experiment to see how how MMO game setting can impact user engagement.

How do I play?

    Goal

      Collect as much honey as possible and build a successful hive

    Commands
    • Move to position - Mouse left click or W A S D
    • Interact with NPC's - Mouse right click or Space
    • Chat with other players - Enter
    • Open Hacker News - N
