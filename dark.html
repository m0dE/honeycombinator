What is this?

    Honey Combinator is an unofficial game designed to explore Y Combinator website. It's a proof of concept for allowing visitors to interact with each other using an embedded HTML5 MMO game.
    *The characters depicted in this game are completely based off of the author's imagination.

How do I play?
    Commands
    • Move to position - Mouse right click or W A S D
    • Interact with NPC's - Mouse left click or Space
    • Chat with other players - Enter
    Goal

      Go to forest and collect honey

Why did you make this?

    I'm trying to figure out a great use case for embeddable MMO games in browser. I think it can improve user engagement, customer support, and brand awareness. If you have any suggestions, please tell me
