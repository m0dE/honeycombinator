What is this?

    Honey Combinator is a proof of concept where you can browse Y Combinator website in a Massively Multiplayer Online (MMO) game world.
    *This project is not affiliated with Y Combinator, and the content & characters depicted are based on the author's imagination.*

Why did you make this?

    I need your feedback! It fascinates me to watch website visitors interacting with each other, and I think there might be a product market fit for this as a promotional/educational tool. Maybe a 404 page could be a MMO game :D
    Do you have a webpage that could use something like this? Hit me up, and I'll build & host a game for free.

    Tell me about your website

Free?! What are you getting out of this?
    I'm building the games using my platform, modd.io. Having these games embedded on various websites will give exposure to my platform. Plus, I'm trying to figure out the product market fit.
Specs?

    • Each server can have 64 concurrent players, and the servers auto scale
    • Our games are HTML5 and are easily embedded using iframe. It can be played in both web and mobile.
    • Let me know if you have any questions! admin@modd.io

    I specialize in 2D multiplayer games, and I can make many different kinds of games such as top-down shooters, MMORPG, mario-style platformer, etc. Here are some of the games that I've made in the past: Braains.io, Escap.io, Salmonz.io, Taekwon.io, Clansmen.io, Too many cooks, and many more..!
